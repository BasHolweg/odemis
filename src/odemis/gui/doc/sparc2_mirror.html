Mirror alignment
  1. Ensure on the SEM image that the mirror hole is roughly centered
  2. Bring the sample less than 1.5 mm from the mirror (by moving the Z axis up), use chamber cam or chamber view if necessary
  3. Turn off all the lights in the chamber
  4. Ensure a CL-emitting part of the sample is at the center of screen
  5. Set the SEM to external scanning (if applicable)
  6. Move X to make the spot image symmetric horizontally
  7. Move Y to reduce the spot size in the vertical direction
  8. Move SEM stage up (Z) to increase spot intensity
  9. Reiterate from step 6
