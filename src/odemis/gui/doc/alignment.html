Quick alignment tips

Dichotomy mode

  1. Adjust SEM to 500x magnification, 30kV and medium current (spot size 3).
  2. Report current SEM magnification and set dwell time to ?? and scale to 8.
  3. Adjust Camera settings until the e-beam is visible in the optical image.
  4. Disable the auto brightness/contrast.
  5. Use Dichotomy mode to select smaller and smaller sub-areas that move the e-beam closer to the center of the optical image.
  6. Click on “move to center”.
  7. Select “spot mode” to verifying that the e-beam can be seen in the optical image.

Spot mode

  1. Set binning of Camera to 1x1
  2. Adjust Camera settings and modify the brightness/contrast until the e-beam is clearly visible.
  3. Double click on the center of the e-beam until it is in the center of the optical image.
  4. Increase SEM magnification to desired level and repeat alignment.
