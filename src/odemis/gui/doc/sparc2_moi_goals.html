Minimum spot intensity required
  • Angular resolved: > 0.1
  • CL intensity: > 0.1
  • Spectrum: > 0.5


Mirror alignment procedure
  1. Turn off all the lights in the chamber
  2. Blank the beam
  3. Acquire background image
  4. Unblank the beam
  5. Move X and Y axis to position the brightest point (moment of inertia) at the center
  6. Move Z axis to increase the moment of inertia and reduce spot size
