Auto focus procedure (Lens)
  1. Turn off all the lights in the chamber
  2. Blank the beam
  3. Press the Auto focus button
  4. Wait until the auto focus procedure is completed


Mirror alignment procedure
  1. Ensure on the SEM image that the mirror hole is roughly correctly centered
  2. Turn off all the lights in the chamber
  3. Blank the beam
  4. Acquire background image
  5. Unblank the beam
  6. Move X and Y axis to position the brightest point (moment of inertia) at the center
  7. Move Z axis to reduce the moment of inertia and increase spot intensity


Lens alignment procedure
  1. Unblank the beam
  2. Move the X axis of the lens to center the CL spot on the blue vertical line
